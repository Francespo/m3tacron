## Objective
The `Include epic content` toggle is currently ignored or improperly handled across pages. Epic-only content should not pollute standard analysis and UI when disabled.

## Context & Symptoms
- If `Include epic content` is disabled (default), any card (ship, pilot, or upgrade) that is playable **only** in the Epic format must be completely excluded from analysis, stats, and UI listings.
- If a card is playable in both Standard and Epic formats, it should remain visible even when Epic is disabled.
- When the toggle is active, include everything.
- Apply this filtering universally across **all pages and analytics**:
  - **Card Browser, Ship Detail, Pilot Detail:** exclude epic-only cards from listings and stats.
  - **Lists & Squadrons:** if a list or squadron contains **any** epic-only ship/pilot, exclude the entire list/squadron from browsers, stats, and aggregates when the toggle is disabled.
